About the Library

LSPU-SCC University Library

LSPU-SCC University Library stands as the main source of academic resources for the students from Laguna State Polytechnic University - Main Campus, a university dedicated to providing high-quality education, distinguished research, sustainable extension, and production services, ensuring continual improvement to meet requirements and deliver efficient and effective services with utmost integrity, professionalism, and innovation.

As a central repository of knowledge, the library embodies the university's dedication to continual improvement. The goal is not only to provide access to a vast array of scholarly materials but also to facilitate an environment that encourages intellectual exploration, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Embodying the university's commitment to holistic education, the LSPU-SCC University Library empowers students to engage in rigorous academic pursuits, stay informed about advancements in their respective fields, and contribute to the university's culture of excellence.

LSPU Vision

LSPU is a center of technology innovation that promotes interdisciplinary learning, sustainable utilization of resources, collaboration and partnership with the community and stakeholders.

LSPU Mission

LSPU, driven by progressive leadership, is a premier institution providing technology-mediated agriculture, fisheries and other related and emerging disciplines significantly contributing to the growth and development of the region and nation.

LSPU Quality Policy

LSPU delivers quality education through responsive instruction, distinctive research, sustainable extension and production service. Thus, we are committed with continual improvement to meet applicable requirements to provide quality, efficient and effective services to the university stakeholder's highest level of satisfaction through an excellent management system imbued with utmost integrity, professionalism and innovation.

LSPU Library Mission

The Library commits itself to fully support the information needs of the academe and the community with an organized, relevant and sufficient collection; and with modernized efficient library service that will meet standards of state-run academic-research libraries.

LSPU Library Vision

The Library's mission is to provide excellent access to information to support the instruction, research and social responsibility of the current programs of the University; and to deliver quality library service through the use of current information and communication technologies (ICT); thereby meeting the needs not only of the academe but of larger community as well.

About the Site

Simplified Login Process

The primary purpose of the site is to simplify the process of entering the LSPU library. The goal is to eliminate or lessen these difficulties with an accessible and straightforward entry process.

Implementation of Technology for Convenience

This site strives to use technology to create a user-friendly solution by implementing features such as a QR code access system and a real-time space availability tracker.

Keeping Track of Visitors

An additional goal is to effectively manage and track student data, ensuring that the system provides accurate information and supports data-driven decision-making for library management.

Enhancing the Student Experiences

This site aims to create an environment where students can maximize their study time, minimize wait times, and access clear information about available spaces. The objective is to elevate the academic journey of LSPU students, ensuring that each library visit is marked by convenience and efficiency.

About the Developers

Why We Exist?

As direct users and main beneficiaries of LSPU's library facilities, we recognized the challenges faced by students in accessing the resources they need. This sparked the idea of leveraging technology to create a solution that goes beyond the use of Google Forms. This web application is not just a project; it's a vision for a more accessible library experience.

Developer's Mission

At the heart of our mission is the commitment to ease library access and provide real-time information on study space availability. We aim to change the way students engage with the LSPU library, creating an environment that can possibly promote convenience, efficiency, and optimal academic success.

Developer's Vision

We aspire to set a new standard for library access at LSPU. By embracing technological advancements, we aim to contribute to a more efficient and conducive space for studying and collaborative work.


We invite you to explore the features of our website and provide feedback to help us continually improve. Your experience matters, and we are dedicated to ensuring that the Student Library Entry Monitoring Website remains a valuable resource for all LSPU students.

Thank you for joining us on this journey towards a more connected and technologically academic community.
